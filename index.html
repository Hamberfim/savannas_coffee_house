Specials

Student Discounts

image source:
https://www.pexels.com/@wildlittlethingsphoto

Show your Student ID card and receive discounts on our daily food and drink specials. The University, Student Union and accredited clubs or organizations may request to use Savanna's Coffee House for small events by filling out our Contact Request Form. There is a $50 reservations fee for each event, which includes moderate facility set-up and clean-up. Additional charges may apply if the events take place outside of regular operating hours. Event organizers may NOT charge admission for events, but donations may be collected. This unique and intimate space is intended for MSU students & faculty and the community and is not available for other commercial type events.
